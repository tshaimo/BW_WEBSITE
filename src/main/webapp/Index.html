Introduction to the Ministry

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs is responsible for promoting and protecting Botswana's interests abroad. We work closely with Botswana's embassies, high commissions, and consulates to advance Botswana's political, economic, and security interests globally.

Our core functions include:

  • Formulating and implementing Botswana's foreign policy
  • Managing Botswana's relations with other countries and international organizations
  • Representing Botswana's interests abroad
  • Providing consular services to Botswana citizens abroad

Our Services

Passport Services

We provide passport services to Botswana citizens, including application, renewal, and replacement of lost or stolen passports.

Visa Services

We provide visa services to foreign nationals traveling to Botswana, including application, issuance, and information on visa requirements.

Consular Services

We provide consular services to Botswana citizens abroad, including assistance with emergencies, legalizations and notarizations, and online services.

About Us

Overview of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs is the government agency responsible for the formulation and implementation of Botswana's foreign policy. The Ministry is headed by the Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation, who is appointed by the President of Botswana.

The Ministry's core functions include:

  • Formulating and implementing Botswana's foreign policy
  • Managing Botswana's relations with other countries and international organizations
  • Representing Botswana's interests abroad
  • Providing consular services to Botswana citizens abroad

History and Milestones

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs was established in 1966, following Botswana's independence from British colonial rule. Since then, the Ministry has played a key role in advancing Botswana's political, economic, and security interests globally.

Some of the Ministry's key milestones include:

  • Establishment of Botswana's first embassies and high commissions abroad in the 1970s
  • Hosting of the 38th Ordinary Summit of the Southern African Development Community (SADC) in 2018
  • Assumption of the Chairmanship of SADC in 2021

Organizational Structure

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs is organized into several departments and divisions, which work together to advance Botswana's foreign policy goals and interests.
